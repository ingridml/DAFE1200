GET TO KNOW US

Attender aims to reduce attendance issues among students in norwegian high schools by combining attendance data collected through location services and technology.

Attender provides insight to attendance for students, teachers and parents, as well as encouraging students to stay in school through motivation-increasing tools, such as fun activities, a "buddy-system" and colloquium groups.
